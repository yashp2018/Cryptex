What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a form of digital currency secured by cryptography and powered by blockchain technology. It enables decentralized, peer-to-peer transactions without relying on banks or governments.

How Can Cryptocurrency Be Used?

  • 💸 Send and receive payments instantly across the globe
  • 💼 Invest, trade, or HODL for potential long-term gains
  • 🔐 Use smart contracts to automate agreements
  • 🛒 Shop online where crypto is accepted
  • 📈 Participate in DeFi (Decentralized Finance) protocols
  • 🎮 Buy in-game items, NFTs, and metaverse assets

Popular Cryptocurrencies

Bitcoin (BTC)
The original cryptocurrency. Often called “digital gold.”
Ethereum (ETH)
Powers smart contracts and decentralized apps (dApps).
BNB (Binance Coin)
Used to pay fees and participate in Binance Smart Chain apps.
Cardano (ADA)
A blockchain focused on sustainability and academic research.
Solana (SOL)
Fast and low-cost blockchain for DeFi and Web3 projects.
Polygon (MATIC)
Ethereum Layer-2 scaling solution for faster transactions.
